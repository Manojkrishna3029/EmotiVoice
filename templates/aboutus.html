Our Mission

Our mission is to harness the power of artificial intelligence and machine learning to accurately detect and interpret human emotions through speech. We aim to contribute to the field of human-computer interaction by providing tools that can enhance emotional intelligence in technology.

Our Story

This project was initiated as a final year project for my postgraduate studies in Computer Applications at Gayatri vidya college for Degree and PG Courses. Driven by a deep interest in AI and its potential to understand human emotions, I embarked on this journey in 2024. Over the course of the project, I have developed a robust system capable of recognizing various emotions in speech with high accuracy.
